# VCU Massey Comprehensive Cancer Center  
## Biostatistics Shared Resource (BSSR)

Welcome to the official GitHub organization of the **Biostatistics Shared Resource (BSSR)** at the VCU Massey Comprehensive Cancer Center.

---

## 🔬 About Us

The **BSSR** promotes excellence in cancer research by providing high-quality biostatistical support and fostering collaborative partnerships with investigators across the cancer center.

Our faculty and staff play a central role in:
- Designing innovative cancer clinical trials  
- Supporting study protocols through PRMS  
- Ensuring study safety and quality via DSMC oversight  
- Contributing to high-impact peer-reviewed research  

We integrate **methodological research, data science, and collaborative expertise** to advance cancer research and improve patient outcomes.

---

To provide outstanding **biostatistical collaboration, methodological innovation, and training** that strengthens cancer research at VCU Massey Comprehensive Cancer Center. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}

## 🎯 Goals

- Foster **long-term collaborations** with cancer investigators from study conception to publication  
- Promote **reproducible and open research practices**  
- Develop and apply **appropriate biostatistical methodologies**  
- Provide training in **study design, analysis, and statistical computing**  

---

## 🤝 Who Should Contact Us

We encourage researchers to engage with us **early in the research process**, including:

- Study design and planning  
- Sample size and power calculations  
- Statistical analysis plans  
- Grant proposals (NIH R, K, P, U series, etc.)  
- Data analysis and interpretation  
- Manuscript preparation  

Early collaboration ensures **higher-quality and more impactful research outcomes**.

---

## ⏳ Recommended Timelines

To ensure high-quality support, we recommend:

- **6 months prior**: Large grants (NIH P/U/SPORE)  
- **2 months prior**: NIH R/K grants  
- **1 month prior**: Abstract-level analyses  

---

## 🔄 Collaboration Process

1. Submit a request through the BSSR portal  
2. Initial consultation (virtual or in-person)  
3. Development of scope-of-work and analysis plan  
4. Formal Statement of Work (SOW)  
5. Project execution and collaboration  
6. Delivery of results and final report  

Collaborations may include:
- Grant writing support  
- Statistical analysis plans  
- Data management strategies  
- Reproducible code and software
